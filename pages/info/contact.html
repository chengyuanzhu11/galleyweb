BITCOIN REWARDS PLATFORM

As a decentralized system,bitcoin operates without a central authority or single administrator,so that anyone can create a new bitcoin address and transact without needing any approval.This is accomplished through a specialized distributed ledger called a blockchain that records bitcoin transactions.

Decentralization

Decentralized blockchains are designed to be unalterable, and once the data is entered it is irreversible. New data can be tacked on, but the old data can't be edited or changed in any way. For Bitcoin, this means transactions are permanently recorded and viewable by anyone.
